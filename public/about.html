About us

Welcome to SCDES an eCommerce website

The Founders of this shop is Ms. Salem Camu Coriño From Tiwi, Albay and this shop was founded on 1996 and later on they established the business name on 2016. The shop makes pottery made of ceramic material with different kinds of uses from flower pots, water pots, cooking pots etc. They also make customized lampshades and trophies with designs and sizes that the buyer wants and needs. They also make unique and elegant wedding giveaways such as little pots that has an engraved names of the bride and groom. This shop is open for made to order that ensures you in every piece is more than just crafted; it's manifestation of your desires, your vision, meticulously designed and brought to life in every customize creation.
